It's Exciting to Share!

Here's a little description of me. I live in Peachtree Corners, a suburb city of Atlanta, GA. I have a cat named Skylar that is delightful company. I have a multitude of friends from my church, Northlands Church, Norcross, GA, so I'm often not home, which makes Skylar very glad to see me. Growing and Going!

Tender Hearts Ministry

I am an ordained pastor and part of my ministry is pastoral counseling. I am the Executive Director of Tender Hearts Ministy, a non-profit organization where I do individual counseling. I also facilitate one day journaling retreats for freedom from childhood emotional wounds that cause pain or discomfort now. I am called by God to bring freedom to His Bride. There is no charge for my ministry services, but gifts are certainly welcome.

Best Impression Colors

You never get a second chance to make a great first impression. When you meet someone, wearing the correct colors brings the focus to your eyes, which is where we connect emotionally with each other. This is so important in a job interview. I analize your skin, hair and eyes to make your personal palette determination. This process includes a case with your color swatches to take with you when you go shopping. It's worth the investment!

Earring Hangers
